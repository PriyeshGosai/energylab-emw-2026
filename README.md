# PyPSA Energy Modelling Workshop

## Overview

This repository contains the training material for the **PyPSA Energy Modelling Workshop** held from **13–15 July 2026** at the **Faculty of Engineering, National University of Laos**, Vientiane.

The workshop is jointly organised by **EnergyLab Asia** and **Climate Compatible Growth (CCG)** to strengthen local capacity in open-source power system modelling. The training introduces participants to **PyPSA (Python for Power System Analysis)**, an open-source framework for modelling electricity systems and supporting operational and long-term energy planning.

Hydropower currently supplies the majority of electricity in Laos. While this provides a low-carbon and flexible power system, seasonal variability and droughts create challenges for energy security. As Laos expands the deployment of solar, wind, and biomass generation, power system modelling becomes increasingly important for evaluating system reliability, renewable integration, and operational strategies.

This workshop focuses on the application of PyPSA to hydropower-dominated electricity systems through a combination of lectures and hands-on exercises using Python and interactive Jupyter notebooks.

## Learning Topics

Participants will explore:

* Power system modelling using Python and PyPSA
* Dispatch planning for chained hydropower systems
* Integration of hydrological inflow data into dispatch models
* Reservoir operation and water management
* Hydropower dispatch optimisation
* Coordination between hydropower and variable renewable energy
* Scenario analysis under high, medium, and low inflow conditions
* Interpretation of model results to support operational decision-making

## Workshop Format

The workshop combines theoretical presentations with practical exercises completed using interactive Python notebooks (Google Colab or a local Python installation). Participants build and analyse energy system models while learning the underlying modelling concepts.

## Target Audience

The workshop is intended for:

* Power system engineers
* Utility and system operator staff
* Government agencies
* Independent Power Producers (IPPs)
* Researchers and academics
* Students and professionals interested in energy system modelling

## Prerequisites

Participants should have:

* A laptop computer
* A Google account (for Google Colab)
* A basic understanding of electricity systems is beneficial but not essential
* No prior experience with PyPSA is required

## About PyPSA

PyPSA (Python for Power System Analysis) is an open-source energy system modelling framework that enables:

* Economic dispatch and optimal power flow
* Capacity expansion planning
* Hydropower and reservoir modelling
* Renewable energy integration studies
* Multi-energy system and sector coupling analysis
* Scenario and sensitivity analysis

PyPSA provides many of the capabilities available in commercial power system modelling software while remaining transparent, flexible, and freely available to users.
